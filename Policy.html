Our Policy – QR Scanify

At QR Scanify, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This application is built with your safety and simplicity in mind.

  • No Data Collection: We do not store or share any scanned or uploaded data.
  • Fully Local Processing: All scans are handled within your device, ensuring your privacy remains intact.
  • No Ads or Trackers: We do not use cookies, trackers, or any form of analytics.

QR Scanify is created only to ease individuals in accessing QR and barcode information quickly, without compromising their privacy.

If you have any questions, feel free to reach out at info@qrscanify.com.
